Manager Bonus

Sr. Manager, Jr. Manager & MIT monthly store performance bonus

Sr. Manager: Vol (1–2%/$3k/$5k) + Close $500 + KPI up to $3k · Requirements: 3/3=100%, 2/3=80%, 1/3=60%, 0/3=50%  |  Jr. Manager: Vol (0.5–1%/$1.5k) + Close $250 + KPI up to $1.5k · Requirements: 2/2=100%, 1/2=80%, 0/2=60%  |  MIT: Vol (0.1–0.2%/$400/$1k) + Close $100 + KPI up to $400 · Requirements: 168 Rebiz hrs  |  Detractors (Sr/Jr): GPPS <$31 or Close Rate <20% = bonus forfeited.
